# Silicon Curious

Silicon Curious is an English blog and podcast brand about AI tools, creative work, and the strange future of everyday technology.

## Positioning

Exploring AI tools, digital culture, and the small experiments that make the future feel less abstract.

## Suggested content tracks

- AI Tools I Actually Use
- Learning in Public
- Notes From the Future

## Launch checklist

- Buy a domain, such as `siliconcurious.com`.
- Create a podcast cover with the words "Silicon Curious".
- Record or generate a short trailer episode.
- Add an AI disclosure when using NotebookLM-generated audio.
- Publish through a podcast host that creates an RSS feed.
- Submit the RSS feed to Apple Podcasts Connect.
